About

Summary

01 year 6+ months experience

I turn complex, role-based user needs into clean, accessible interfaces — moving from low-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity Figma prototypes without losing sight of the person actually using the product. My background is B2B SaaS: dashboards, monitoring tools, and field-operations software where clarity and information hierarchy matter more than decoration.

I work closely with product managers and engineers inside Agile sprints, and I care about the handoff as much as the design itself — annotated specs, consistent components, and documentation that reduces back-and-forth with developers.
