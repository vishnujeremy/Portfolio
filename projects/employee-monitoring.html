Overview

What it does

This platform gives organizations one place to monitor employee activity, measure productivity, manage company assets, analyze work performance, and generate business insights. It supports multiple organizational roles — Super Admin, Company Admin, Managers, and Employees — each with access scoped to what they actually need. Monitoring, productivity analytics, attendance tracking, KPI management, remote administration, and reporting all live in a single unified product.

My role

What I designed

I worked on the product from concept through to high-fidelity UI — designing user flows, information architecture, wireframes, UI components, the design system, responsive layouts, and interactive Figma prototypes for scalable dashboards across every user role. Simplicity and usability were the priority throughout: enterprise-level functionality, without an enterprise-level learning curve.

Design highlights

How I approached it

  • Designed using a clean, minimal enterprise dashboard approach rather than the dense, alert-heavy look typical of monitoring software.
  • Built reusable design system components so the product could scale without visual drift as modules were added.
  • Created intuitive navigation to keep large-scale enterprise modules easy to find, not just technically organized.
  • Applied clear visual hierarchy to simplify large datasets — KPI cards and summarized metrics over raw tables wherever possible.
  • Optimized workflows separately for administrators, managers, and employees, since each group needed a different level of detail.
  • Designed fully responsive layouts to hold up across different screen sizes.
